TRADITIONAL WORK

I have been drawing since childhood and my love for art has only grown with time. Traditional work has always been my foundation, giving me the freedom to explore creativity in many ways. My work includes detailed fantasy inking, playful doodles, oil paintings, tattoo designs and character drawings. I also enjoy creating cartoons and imaginative figures. Over the years, I have cleared all my basic drawings through traditional practice and I still revisit fundamentals like gesture drawing and basic shapes from time to time. For me, traditional art is more than just a skill. It is a lifelong journey that continues to shape how I create and share my vision with others.
